Estate Coin Cleanouts – I Handle the Heavy, Messy Part

If you’re dealing with an estate, inheritance, or house cleanout anywhere between Santa Rosa, Ukiah, and the coast, the coins are usually the last thing anyone wants to touch.

Coffee cans, jars, buckets, bags, desk drawers full of change – it’s heavy, it’s dirty, and it’s slow to deal with. That’s where I come in.

  • I come to the property or meet nearby, where practical in my service area.
  • I look over the coins in bulk – you don’t have to pre-sort anything.
  • I make a clear offer based on volume, condition, and how much work is involved.
  • You don’t have to sort, roll, or haul anything to the bank.
